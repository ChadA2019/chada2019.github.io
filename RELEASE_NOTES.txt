BalanceIQ v7.7 — GST Plausibility and Digit Repair

- Repairs OCR GST strings with one inserted digit, such as $211.55 or $214.55, back to $21.55 when consistent with Total/11.
- Prevents damaged printed GST values such as $21.70 from overriding a strongly supported expected GST.
- Retains v7.6 receipt-number recovery and all prior total/date improvements.

BalanceIQ v7.7 — Bunnings Footer Invoice Recovery

- Recovers Bunnings receipt numbers from partial barcode/footer references such as #019-78347-2052 even when the following year/date is merged or corrupted.
- Prevents product barcodes from being reconstructed as receipt numbers.
- Restricts digit-based invoice reconstruction to text immediately associated with an Invoice Number label.
- Retains all v7.5 total, GST and date improvements.

BalanceIQ v7.7
Build 2026.07.21.024

- A printed two-decimal amount on an explicitly labelled Total line is now authoritative.
