BalanceIQ v9.0 — Dual Local OCR Release

- Added official PaddleOCR.js PP-OCRv5 as the primary full-receipt reader.
- Retained Tesseract.js multi-pass OCR for focused date, totals, GST and amount verification.
- PaddleOCR word coordinates are reconstructed into visual rows before parsing.
- Added dynamic PaddleOCR summary-row detection around Total, GST, EFT, Card, Change and related labels.
- PaddleOCR and Tesseract evidence are merged into the existing receipt parser.
- Graceful fallback: if PaddleOCR cannot load, Tesseract continues to work.
- All inference occurs on the user's device. There is no paid API and no receipt-processing backend.
