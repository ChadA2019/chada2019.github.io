BalanceIQ v9.9.0 — Local-First Device Storage

- Replaces localStorage-only persistence with IndexedDB as the primary on-device database.
- Automatically migrates existing BalanceIQ data into the device database.
- Keeps a lightweight localStorage mirror as a compatibility fallback.
- Adds configurable automatic rolling safety snapshots with 5, 10 or 20 retained copies.
- Adds manual snapshot creation and latest-snapshot recovery.
- Adds storage usage, last-save and recovery status to Settings.
- Adds a browser persistent-storage request to reduce automatic eviction risk where supported.
- Retains downloadable JSON backups and encrypted cloud sync for protection beyond one device.

Important: clearing site storage, uninstalling the PWA, device loss or factory reset can still remove local-only data. Downloaded backups or encrypted cloud sync remain recommended.
